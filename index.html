About CivicPulse

CivicPulse pulls from publicly available US government APIs every day at 07:00 UTC — legislation from congress.gov, campaign donations from the Federal Election Commission, and headline coverage from NewsAPI.

No spin, no commentary. Raw public record, presented plainly.
