About CivicPulse

CivicPulse pulls from publicly available US government APIs every day at 07:00 UTC and presents the data in one place — no spin, no commentary.

Data sources: congress.gov for legislation and membership, the Federal Election Commission for campaign finance, and NewsAPI for headline coverage.
